Our Mission

We are a team of four dedicated developers seeking to improve the lives of our fellow food enthusiasts who suffer from gluten-digestion issues. People who suffer from celiac disease must avoid any food items containing barley, rye, and wheat. Currently, the only treatment is a lifelong adherence to a strict gluten-free diet. As a result, finding edible food can become a chore. This is where we come in! Our users are presented with an immediate list of all nearby gluten-safe restaurants. No additional searches or filters needed. Users can rest easy or, rather, eat easy knowing their choice of food has been approved by the gluten-free community!
